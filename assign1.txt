1- Whats the difference between Div and Section?

<div> is a generic container used for layout and styling purposes without any semantic meaning.

<section> is a semantic element used to define thematic groupings of content within a document.


2- Whats the difference between Article and P?

<p> is for individual paragraphs of text.

<article> is for self-contained, reusable content like blog posts or articles.
