Vegetable Showcase

Carrot

Carrots are a root vegetable. They are rich in vitamin A, which is good for eyesight.

Broccoli

Broccoli is a cruciferous vegetable. It is rich in vitamin C and fiber, and is known for its cancer-fighting properties.

Tomato

Tomatoes are technically a fruit, but they are commonly used as a vegetable in cooking. They are rich in lycopene, which is good for heart health.
